Welcome to the Green Garden Cafe and Deli website! This cafe is run by Mindy and Mike. They have started a nonprofit organization to help combat food waste created by the local major supermarkets and distributors in Manchester.
We have set up a “pay as you feel” food box that is full of fresh, diverted food from the grocery stores and farmers markets available from select pick up locations within the city on certain days. This is a limited item, so on-line reservations for a weekly box is essential.
Our current list of specials we are serving is listed below.
  • Our current list of specials:
  • Pesto, tomato and mozzarella focaccia with a rocket salad
  • Spicy tomato and mascapone soup served with crusty brown bread
  • A classic ceasar salad served with courgette chips
  • Spinach and pine nut and smoked salmon tart
Our cafe is located on Hilton Street in Manchester's Northern Quarter. The location of food box pick ups will be posted on our Facebook page and will be regularly updated!
Our opening hours are Monday-Saturday 9am-4:30pm and Sunday 10:30am-4pm
Hot food is served from 10am Monday-Saturday and from 11am on Sundays
